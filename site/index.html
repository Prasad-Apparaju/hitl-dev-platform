The system at a glance

The three commitments behind every page here

  • Documentation is the shared memory. Every AI session loads the same conventions, boundaries, and decisions — the hundredth change is as grounded as the first.
  • Gates are structural, not heroic. Enforcement hooks block ungoverned edits, tests are reviewed before code exists, and production deploys are gated on verified readiness — by scripts that fail closed, not by discipline.
  • Every claim leaves evidence. Decision records, test and incident registries, a readiness register, and a traceability chain from requirement to deploy: "was this reviewed?" has a checkable answer.
